Student. Father. Son. Husband.

Hello. My name is Matthew Wotton. I am a Gold Coast native, closer to 40 then I am 30. I am married and have two young boys, plus our dog Aisha.

I am an Artisan Baker who specialises in sourdough bread, I have been a baker for the better part of 15 years.

As my family grows and priorities change, I am now embarking on a career change. I am passionate about baking however I am trying to create a better work / life balance.

In 2021 to facilitate my goal of changing careers, I enrolled into a Bachelor of Information Technology through RMIT part time while still working full time and enjoying the role of being a new father.

I was drawn to I.T for two reasons. Firstly, the world is moving towards being more towards the online space with people and businesses spending more time and more money in the area. Secondly, I believe learning the backbones of I.T will put me in a better position to be more employable as I would not only have the skills and experiences of my work and life history but I would now have the knowledge and skills required for the future.
